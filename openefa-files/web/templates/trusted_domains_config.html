About Trusted Domains
What are Trusted Domains?

Trusted domains are sources that have been verified as legitimate and low-risk. Emails from these domains receive expedited processing and reduced security scrutiny.

When to Add a Domain
  • Major service providers - Microsoft, Google, Zoom, etc.
  • Financial institutions - Banks, payment processors, credit card companies
  • Government agencies - .gov, .mil domains
  • Business partners - Regular vendors with verified authentication
Security Considerations
Important: Only add domains you absolutely trust. Free email providers (gmail.com, outlook.com) should generally NOT be trusted as they can be used by anyone. Use sender-specific whitelisting for individual users instead.
Examples of Good Trusted Domains
  • microsoft.com - Microsoft corporate services
  • microsoftonline.com - Microsoft authentication
  • chase.com - Chase Bank official domain
  • zoom.us - Zoom Video Communications
  • usdoj.gov - US Department of Justice
